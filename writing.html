Writing

I believe this is my first argumentative essay I’ve written, and it has strict requirements. I had orginally picked an anti-gun control position, listing how many guns were used for defence but my arguments were refuted. I had changed then my position into pro-gun control. The research I had done took some time but it was worth doing. A major problem I have with this essay is that it sounds like it’s reading off statistics. I’d put more emotion into next time, and relate it to something relative at the time.

This is the sort of essay I like writing, and I think it’s still decent. The introduction and conclusion are a well enough written summary of my thoughts. The problems I have would be the body paragraphs. They feel like plot synopsis rather than actual analysis.
